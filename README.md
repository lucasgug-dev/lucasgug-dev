## About

I am a Computer Science student at the **University of Innsbruck** with a foundation in **electronics and computer engineering** from HTL Anichstrasse. I build software with an engineering-first mindset: clear architecture, reliable interfaces, thoughtful trade-offs, and solutions grounded in real user needs.

My current work spans **Java and Spring Boot backends**, **React-based interfaces**, **REST API design**, **SQL**, and containerized development with **Docker**. Alongside full-stack engineering, I am developing practical **AI/ML expertise** with Python and data-oriented languages, connecting software fundamentals with intelligent, product-ready systems.

I bring the discipline of hands-on technical work, structured problem-solving, and experience operating in team environments. My goal is to grow into a software and AI engineer who can take a product from problem definition through implementation, validation, and continuous improvement.
